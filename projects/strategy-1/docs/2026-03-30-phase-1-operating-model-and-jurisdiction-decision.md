# Strategy #1 Phase-1 Operating Model and Jurisdiction Decision
Date: 2026-03-30
Owner: Tanya (CEO)

## Decision Summary
This memo records CEO approval decisions for the Phase-1 legal operating perimeter for Strategy #1.

## 1) Launch Capital Model
Decision: Approved

- Phase 1 launch is approved as proprietary-capital only.
- No customer funds, customer order handling, managed accounts, or agency execution are authorized in Phase 1.

## 2) Approved and No-Go Jurisdictions (Phase 1)
Decision: Approved with restrictions

- Approved jurisdictions for live operations in Phase 1:
  - United States only, subject to venue and contractual eligibility checks.
- No-go jurisdictions for Phase 1:
  - Any non-US onboarding or distribution.
  - Any jurisdiction restricted by sanctions/export-control rules or venue contractual limitations.

## 3) Customer-Capital Features Scope
Decision: Rejected for Phase 1

- Customer-capital features remain out of scope for initial launch.
- Any move to customer-capital, advisory, managed-capital, or custody-adjacent workflows requires a separate legal workstream and board-approved go/no-go decision.

## 4) Expansion Review Timeline (Leverage, Derivatives, Staking, Lending)
Decision: Deferred pending controls and legal package

- 2026-04-10: Complete Phase-1 perimeter signoff (Legal, Engineering, CEO) for proprietary-only launch.
- 2026-04-17: Complete control-evidence package (audit logs, access logging, retention, surveillance controls).
- 2026-05-01: Start formal expansion legal review for leverage/derivatives/staking/lending.
- 2026-05-15: CEO + board checkpoint to decide whether any expansion path can move to implementation planning.
- Earliest target for implementation planning after checkpoint: 2026-05-16, contingent on legal and control readiness.

## Operating Conditions
- Expansion requests must pass legal gate review before engineering implementation.
- New jurisdiction, new venue, and new product type changes require explicit legal signoff.
- Material exceptions require documented CEO approval and board visibility.

## Effective Date
This decision is effective as of 2026-03-30.
